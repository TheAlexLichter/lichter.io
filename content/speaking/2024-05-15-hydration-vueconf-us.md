After Vue.js Amsterdam earlier in the year, the hydration talk made its way across the Atlantic to VueConf US in New Orleans.

We went back to basics on why hydration exists, what Vue actually does during that phase and - the fun part - what it looks like when hydration goes sideways. Attendees left with a mental model of the process that makes the typical hydration errors far less mysterious to debug.
