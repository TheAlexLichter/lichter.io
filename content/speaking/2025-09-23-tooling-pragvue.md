Back at PragVue, but this time as the opening keynote and with a very different topic than last year's composable best practices.

I took the Prague Vue community on a tour through the current state of JavaScript tooling - Vite, Rolldown, Oxc, Oxlint - and explained why this new generation of tools, many of them built in Rust, matters for Vue and Nuxt developers specifically.
