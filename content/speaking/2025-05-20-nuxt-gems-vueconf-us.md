My first time bringing the "Gems of Nuxt" talk to VueConf US! The lineup of gems received a refresh for 2025, covering less-obvious Nuxt features and patterns that can make your code shorter, safer or both.

As always, these aren't hypothetical niceties - each of them is something I've recommended (and often implemented) in client projects and open-source work. A good chance for the VueConf US audience to take a few underrated features home and start using them in their own Nuxt apps.
