My first time speaking at Vue Fes Japan in Tokyo! I brought the "Gems of Nuxt" talk overseas and walked the audience through 8 lesser-known Nuxt features and patterns (different ones than in the previous talks of course!).

While Nuxt 4's headline updates get most of the attention, this talk focused on the practical gems that deliver tangible benefits day-to-day: less boilerplate, more expressive code, unlocked potential and, simply, fewer bugs. Each feature was one I'd actually recommended to client projects, not just a theoretical curiosity.
