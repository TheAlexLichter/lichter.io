What do modern frontend frameworks have in common? Increasingly, the answer is: the same tooling underneath.

In this Frontend Nation 2025 talk, I introduced the audience to the new generation of JavaScript tooling coming out of VoidZero and the wider ecosystem - Rolldown as a Rust-powered bundler with Rollup compatibility, Oxc as the toolchain it builds upon, Oxlint, and where this is all heading in terms of a unified, faster developer experience across frameworks.
