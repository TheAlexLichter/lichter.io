A playful, Monty-Python-inspired look at the Vue ecosystem: What *has* Vue ever done for you? Turns out - quite a lot.

From the Composition API to Pinia, from Vite (yes, really) to Volar, from Nuxt to countless OSS packages - this talk is a celebration of how much the Vue community has shipped over the years and how these pieces show up in your day-to-day work, even if you don't always realize it.
