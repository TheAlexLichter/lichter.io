Composables are one of the greatest strengths of Vue's Composition API - but writing *good* composables is not as straightforward as it seems. Too often, they turn into bloated utility functions that are hard to reuse, test, or maintain.

In this talk at the very first PragVue, I distilled the patterns I use in client projects and in the Nuxt ecosystem into a set of practical best practices that help you design composables that are reusable, predictable and a joy to work with.
