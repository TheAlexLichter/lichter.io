My first time at Vue.js Paris! The meetup was kindly hosted by Algolia and the theme of the evening was server components and blazingly fast toolchains.

I walked the Paris audience through what Nuxt 4 makes possible today, how Rolldown and Oxc plug into that story and why this new generation of Rust-powered tooling matters for Vue and Nuxt developers specifically.
