Following the launch of Monterail's State of Vue.js 2025 Report at Vue.js Amsterdam, I joined Evan You, Daniel Roe, Daniel Kelly, and Louëlla Creemers on stage for a panel discussion.

We shared our take on the challenges Vue and Nuxt developers face today - from Vue 2 to Vue 3 migration strategies, ecosystem tooling, and when *not* to upgrade, to best practices we see teams get right (and wrong) in production.
