Nuxt has come a long way: From a small meta-framework on top of Vue 2 to a full-stack solution that powers countless production apps. But where does it go from here?

In this talk, we'll take a tour through the current state of Nuxt 3, the ecosystem around it, and a sneak peek at what's on the horizon (Nuxt 4) and why it hasn't arrived yet. Expect a healthy mix of features that already shape the developer experience today and hints towards future versions of Nuxt that will push the framework further.
