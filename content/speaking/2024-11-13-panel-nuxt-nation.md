Alongside Sébastien Chopin, Pooya Parsa, and Daniel Roe, I joined the Nuxt Core Team panel at Nuxt Nation 2024.

We discussed the roadmap towards Nuxt 4, what the core goals for the developer experience are, how compatibility templates and experimental APIs help teams migrate smoothly, and where the framework (and ecosystem) is heading next.
